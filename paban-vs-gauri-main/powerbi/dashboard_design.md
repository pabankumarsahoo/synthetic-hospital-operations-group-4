# Power BI Dashboard Design

Page name: Hospital Ops Command Center

Top KPI cards:
- Total ER Visits
- Average ER Wait Minutes
- Bed Occupancy %
- Patients Per Staff

Main visuals:
- Gauge: Average Occupancy Rate, target 85%, danger above 90%.
- Line chart: Average ER Wait Minutes by hour.
- Clustered bar: Patients Per Staff by shift and department.
- Heatmap/matrix: Department vs shift, colored by occupancy rate.
- Table: Department, shift, visits, avg wait, occupancy, staff count, load status.

Filters:
- Date
- Department
- Shift
- Staff Role

Recommended colors:
- Normal: green
- High load: amber
- Critical: red
- Neutral background with hospital-style blue accents
